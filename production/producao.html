Making Of Arcane

Check out behind the scenes on youtube

The creation of Arcane was nothing short of groundbreaking, blending traditional storytelling with cutting-edge animation techniques. Produced by Riot Games and Fortiche Production, the series stands out for its hand-painted textures and intricate blend of 2D and 3D animation. The team aimed to create a visually stunning world that remained emotionally grounded, ensuring every frame felt like a piece of art. The production process involved close collaboration between animators, writers, and game developers to stay true to the League of Legends universe while crafting a narrative accessible to newcomers. Motion capture was used extensively to bring lifelike performances to the characters, capturing subtle emotions and movements. The animators then enhanced these performances with expressive details unique to the medium.
