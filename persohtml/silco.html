Silco

Zaun

Silco, a central antagonist in Arcane, is a cunning and ruthless leader from Zaun, driven by a vision of independence for his city. Once a close ally of Vander, Silco’s path diverged as he became disillusioned with the idea of peaceful coexistence with Piltover. Instead, he seeks to break free from the oppressive control of the upper city, willing to use any means necessary to achieve his goal. Silco’s ambition is matched only by his manipulative nature and unyielding desire for power, making him a dangerous and unpredictable force in Zaun’s struggle for freedom.
